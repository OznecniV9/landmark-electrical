Our Story

Landmark Electrical Contractors, LLC started in New Britain, CT with a simple promise: do the job right, keep the site clean, and stand behind the work. For decades we’ve handled complex commercial projects—tight timelines, strict compliance, and zero shortcuts.

Now we’re bringing that same standard back to homeowners. Whether it’s fixing a tripping breaker or upgrading your entire service, you’ll get professional-grade workmanship and clear communication at every step.
